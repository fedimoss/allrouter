# AllRouter Privacy Policy

Thank you for using AllRouter Services. Protecting your privacy is critically important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information. Please read it carefully to understand our data processing practices.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- Account Information: When you register an Account, we collect basic information such as your name, email address, and password.

- Usage Data: We record how you use our Services, including the number of API calls, the types of models used, and the time of each call.

- Payment Information: If you subscribe to our paid Services, we collect the payment information necessary to process your transaction, but we do not store complete credit card information.

- Technical Data: We may collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and device information to optimize our Services.

## 2. How We Use Information

We primarily use the information we collect to:

- Provide, maintain, and improve our Services

- Process your transactions and manage your Account

- Send you technical notices, updates, security alerts, and support messages

- Respond to your comments, questions, and requests

- Monitor and analyze usage trends to improve the user experience

- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 3. Data Transmission and Security

**Important Notice: AllRouter is an API service provider. We do not store content data that you transmit through the API. We act solely as an intermediary that forwards your requests to the relevant AI model provider.**

We take the following measures to protect your data:

- Use encryption technologies to protect data in transit

- Implement strict access controls to limit employee access to User Data

- Regularly review and update our security measures

- Comply with applicable data protection laws and regulations

## 4. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- Service Providers: We may share information with third parties that provide services to us, such as payment processors and customer support providers.

- Compliance and Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information when required by law or as necessary to protect our rights.

- Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

- With Your Consent: After obtaining your consent, we may use or share your information for purposes not covered by this Privacy Policy.

## 5. Your Rights

Subject to applicable data protection laws, you may have the following rights:

- Access your personal information

- Correct inaccurate information

- Delete your personal information

- Restrict or object to the processing of your information

- Data portability

- Withdraw consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us.

## 6. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect and store information in order to provide a better user experience. You can control the use of cookies through your browser settings.

## 7. Third-Party Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services that have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties and recommend that you review their privacy policies.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that we may have collected a child's personal information, please contact us immediately.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new version on our website. We recommend that you review this page periodically for the latest information.
